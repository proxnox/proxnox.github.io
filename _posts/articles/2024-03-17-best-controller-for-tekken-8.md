## Types of Controllers
When it comes to choosing a controller for Tekken 8, you have several options to consider. Let's take a closer look at the different types of controllers available and their pros and cons:

### Arcade Sticks
Arcade sticks are a popular choice among Tekken 8 players, especially those who frequent arcades or enjoy the nostalgic feel of playing on an authentic arcade machine. These controllers feature a joystick and buttons, mimicking the setup of traditional arcade cabinets. Arcade sticks offer precise inputs and a robust build quality, making them ideal for intense gaming sessions. However, they can have a steeper learning curve for beginners and may require additional customization for optimal performance.

![arcade stick](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/73/Neo-Geo-AES-Controller-FL.jpg/1024px-Neo-Geo-AES-Controller-FL.jpg)

### Pads
Pads, or gamepads, are the standard controllers that come with gaming consoles like the PlayStation 5 (PS5) and Xbox Series X. They offer a familiar and comfortable grip, making them a popular choice for casual gamers. Pads typically have a D-pad and face buttons, providing a straightforward layout for executing moves in Tekken 8. While pads may lack the arcade-like feel of arcade sticks, they offer convenience and ease of use. However, they may not provide the same level of precision as arcade sticks for advanced techniques.

![ps4 controller](https://images.pexels.com/photos/2323435/pexels-photo-2323435.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1)

### Leverless Options (Arcade Stick wihtout joystick)
Leverless controllers, also known as [**hitbox**](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=hitbox){:target="_blank"} or [**mixbox**](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=mixbox){:target="_blank"}, offer an alternative to traditional arcade sticks. These controllers replace the joystick with buttons for directional inputs, allowing for precise and consistent execution of moves. Leverless options provide a unique experience and can be beneficial for players who struggle with joystick inputs. However, they may require some adjustment for players accustomed to using a joystick and may not be as widely available as arcade sticks or pads.
